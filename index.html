Tim Berners-Lee is a software enjineer who invented the Internet(or world wide web) in 1989 at CERN, the European Particle Physics Laboratory. He is the director of the World Wide Web Consortium that he founded and a professor in the Computer Science Department at the University of Oxford. In 2001 he became a Fellow Of the RoyalSociety and in 2004 he was knighted.

In 1994 Berners-Lee founded the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) at the Laboratory of Computer Science(LCS) at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in Bonston. It was made of companies that wanted to work together to create Web standards and also improve the Web. I was quickly aggreed that the Technologyshould be free of royalties and patents. Berners-Lee has served as director of W3C since it was founded. In 1995 Microsoft produced the first version of internet explorer and this commercial browse created enormous growth in websited and web users. The Web was first used for e-commerce in 1997.
